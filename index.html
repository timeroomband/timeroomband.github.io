ABOUT

Time Room is an art punk/garage rock trio hailing from Minneapolis. Led by vocalist/guitarist Ben Richer, and joined by Kyle Kimbllin on bass and Ian Sutherland on drums, Time Room’s sound blends heavy riffs, hardcore tempos, and danceable rhythms: like Television meets Minor Threat in the green room of a Rush concert.

Their debut album, Something New, is available now.
